What Samson does

Practical home repairs and finishing work from one local contact

The service is designed for homeowners, landlords and small businesses that need repairs, decorating, tiling, flooring, furniture assembly, wall mounting or general property maintenance handled carefully.

Quotes are discussed clearly before work starts. Photos, measurements, location and access details help confirm whether the job is suitable and make the estimate more accurate.

Where regulated or specialist work is required, the job is assessed first so a dedicated qualified trade specialist can be used instead.
