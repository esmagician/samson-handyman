Service overview

Useful for splashbacks, bathroom areas, repairs and neat edge finishing

This service is aimed at smaller tiling jobs and finishing work where you want a careful local handyman rather than a large project team. It works well for kitchen splashbacks, bathroom updates, replacement tiles, grout improvement work and tidy edge details that improve the look of the space.

If the area needs preparation first, the work can also include basic readiness tasks around the same space so the final finish feels more complete and practical.

Common tiling and grout work Samson can help with

Kitchen splashbacks

A common fit for this service. Splashback tiling around worktops, units and socket positions is handled with a focus on neat cuts and a clean final line.

Bathroom and utility areas

Smaller bathroom walls, utility spaces and areas that need regrouting or a tidier finish can be assessed without turning the job into a full refit.

Repair and refresh work

Loose, cracked or tired-looking sections can often be improved with replacement tiles, grout refresh work and finishing updates where the layout still makes sense.
