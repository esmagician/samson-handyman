Privacy Policy

Last updated: 29 June 2026

Information collected

When you submit a quote request, Samson Handyman may collect your name, phone number, email address, area, service interest, job details and any information you choose to include.

How information is used

Your information is used to respond to your enquiry, provide quotes, arrange work, keep business records and understand which advertising or website pages led to enquiries.

Advertising measurement

This website may use Google Ads tags to measure actions such as form submissions, phone clicks and WhatsApp clicks. If you arrive from an advert, click identifiers such as GCLID, GBRAID or WBRAID may be stored and submitted with your enquiry so later qualified or booked leads can be measured in Google Ads.

Sharing and processors

Enquiries may be processed using email, website hosting, form handling tools, Google Ads and related measurement tools. Your enquiry details are not sold.

Your choices

You can request access, correction or deletion of your enquiry information by emailing samsonashandyman@gmail.com.
