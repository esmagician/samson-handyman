Service overview

Local decorating help without needing a full large contractor job

This service works well for homeowners, landlords and local businesses that need a room, hallway, stairwell or smaller property area prepared and finished properly. The focus is on a neat result, sensible prep and clear communication before work starts.

Jobs can include room refreshes between tenants, repainting tired walls and ceilings, woodwork updates, feature walls, touch-ups after repairs, and decorating work that sits alongside other handyman finishing tasks.

What is included

Common painting and decorating work Samson can help with

Preparation first

Surface preparation matters. Filling, sanding, masking, dust-sheet protection and priming are handled carefully so the finish lasts better and the room is left tidy.

Interior and woodwork

Walls, ceilings, skirting boards, door frames, doors, window boards and smaller decorative details can all be included depending on the job.

Refresh work and touch-ups

Not every job needs a full property repaint. Smaller room refreshes, repair touch-ups and problem areas can be assessed on their own.
