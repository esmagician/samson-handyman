Practical support

Small maintenance jobs should not delay a tenancy

Samson Handyman helps letting agents, property managers and landlords deal with the practical repairs that sit between a tenant report and a specialist contractor.

The service is designed for planned work: clear job lists, straightforward access arrangements and tidy completion across homes in Reading, Twyford and nearby towns.

For regulated, specialist or emergency work, the request is assessed first so the correct qualified trade can be used where required.

Before sending a job

Questions from agents and property managers

What work can I request?

Repair lists, decorating touch-ups, wall repairs, fittings, minor plumbing, flooring, tiling, assembly and practical changeover work.

Can I group several jobs together?

Yes. Send a list grouped by property, with photos and access details. The scope can then be reviewed as one visit where practical.

Can you attend occupied properties?

Occupied and vacant properties can be considered. Include the tenant or access contact and preferred appointment windows.

Do you provide emergency cover?

No. This service is for planned maintenance, repair lists and property refreshes rather than 24-hour emergency call-outs.
